01 · Prompt arrival

“Every conversation begins with a prompt.”

The instant you press Enter, your words leave the input box and begin a journey measured in milliseconds.

02 · Tokenisation

Language becomes tokens

Models don’t read words the way we do. Your prompt is split into tokens — small fragments of text, each mapped to a number — before any processing begins.

03 · Understanding

Concepts light up

Inside the network, tokens activate patterns learned from vast amounts of text. Related ideas — language, logic, code, creativity — illuminate together, forming a rich representation of what you meant.

04 · Planning

The AI operations centre
